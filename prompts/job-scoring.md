Score every internship offer using a 100-point fit model:

- location fit: 20
- role fit: 20
- industry fit: 15
- experience fit: 20
- language fit: 10
- timing and duration fit: 10
- offer quality and brand relevance: 5

Also estimate:
- probability of interview
- candidate strengths
- candidate risks
- best application angle
- short LinkedIn message
- cover letter hook

Use realistic judgment. A prestigious company is not enough if the role, language, seniority or location is wrong.
