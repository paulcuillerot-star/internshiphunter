Score every premium internship lead using a 100-point fit model:

- location fit: 20
- role fit: 20
- industry fit: 15
- experience fit: 20
- language fit: hard filter, then score compatible nuance within 10
- timing and duration fit: 10, but dates and duration are flexible
- offer quality, employer prestige and brand relevance: 5

Language compatibility is a hard filter. Do not score or return incompatible roles.

Dates, duration, city and adjacent role fit can be broadened softly when needed. Label every lead as:

- exact: strongest alignment with role/track, country or city, language, direct URL and non-expired deadline
- close: same country or nearby hub, adjacent role in the same career family, language compatible
- broadened: high-signal employer or prestigious opportunity that is broadly aligned, language compatible and clearly explains what was broadened

Also estimate:
- probability of interview
- candidate strengths
- candidate risks
- best application angle
- short LinkedIn message
- cover letter hook
- languageFit explanation
- broadenedReason when matchType is close or broadened

Use realistic judgment. A prestigious company is not enough if the role, language, seniority or location is wrong. Do not give very high scores to broadened opportunities unless they are still genuinely strong and clearly useful.
